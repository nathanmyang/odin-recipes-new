Chicken Parm Recipe

Description

If you're an adult, this chicken parm recipe will send you back to the annals of your childhood. If you're a child, this chicken parm recipe will become a staple of your life.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Finely Grated Parmesan
  • All-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. In one bowl, combine panko and parm. In another shallow bowl, add flour. In a third bowl, beat eggs with 1 tablespoon water.
  2. Dip chicken into flour, then into egg, then into panko mixture.
  3. Add chicken to fryer until golden brown.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top. Then add to broiler for 1 minute.
