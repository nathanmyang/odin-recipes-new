Lasagna

Description

Recipe for a delicious lasagna, guaranteed to make your tastebuds water.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beschemel Sauce
  • Parmasan Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Add boiled lasagna noodle to bottom of casserole dish
  2. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top
  3. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce to layer
  4. Repeat the same layers two more times
