Marinara Sauce

Description

This marinara sauce is a wonderful marriage between tomato and basil flavors. It can be used in all varieties of pasta recipes and more.

Ingredients

  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Dried Chili (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and onion and saute in dutch oven for 3-5 minutes, or until fragrant
  2. Add Canned Tomato sauce
  3. Cook on low heat for 3-5 hours lid-on, stirring occassionally.
  4. Before serving, add Basil for additional and fresh flavor.
